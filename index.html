Job update

For the past 2 months I've been working on automating the datacenter infrastructure for our GPU nodes. I started looking for an automating tool that could both support XenServer and other Linux distributions without a problem. I stumbled upon Razor, a package from the guys behind Puppet, which allows me to easily configure policies and other configurations to PXE boot our physical hardware over in our datacenter without being there. Since Razor only takes care of the OS installation and basic configurations of a machine we needed another tool to automate the provisioning of the machines. This is were Chef came into play where I've been working with for the past few weeks, customising a lot of stuff and writing ruby code.
